Online food delivery platform
Example: Doordash
This type of business connects users with a variety of local restaurants, and connects delivery personnel with different orders. The platform differentiates itself through personalized features including allergen tracking, prime club membership, customizable favorite lists, and family account management. Dynamic business features such as time-bound promotions and surge pricing allow restaurants to optimize engagement and profitability. Users can browse restaurants by categories, view detailed menus organized into sections and dishes, and place order. Orders progress through detailed status stages, and are fulfilled by a fleet of delivery personnel tracked by availability and vehicle type, with their performance recorded in a delivery history log. Users can rate not only restaurants but also individual dishes and delivery service, while restaurants can respond directly to reviews. The system supports secure payments, real-time tracking, promotional codes, and supports reviews and replies to ensure feedback loops.
