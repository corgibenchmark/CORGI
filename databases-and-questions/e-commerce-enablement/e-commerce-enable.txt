E-commerce enablement platform
Example: Shopify
This type of business provides an end-to-end platform that enables entrepreneurs, SMBs, and large enterprises to launch, operate, and scale online storefronts with minimal technical complexity. The platform offers drag-and-drop website builders, integrated payment processing, inventory and order management, and multi-channel sales capabilities across marketplaces, social media, and in-person POS systems. It integrates data from product catalogs, customer interactions, and transaction histories to support a seamless retail experience. Merchants can run marketing campaigns, personalize product recommendations, and automate workflows using built-in analytics and third-party apps. The platform supports real-time inventory syncing, flexible fulfillment options, and automated tax and shipping calculations. Businesses can enhance customer engagement through CRM tools, loyalty programs, and abandoned cart recovery.
