UX-driven advertising analytics platform 
Example: ux ad
This type of business provides a full-stack data platform focused on optimizing digital advertising and customer experience through behavioral analytics, A/B testing, and multi-channel attribution. The platform integrates data from advertisement impressions, clicks, user sessions, content views, and CRM records to offer a unified view of the customer journey. Advertisers and product teams can launch campaigns, define audience segments, and test multiple ad creatives or landing pages using built-in experimentation tools. The platform supports real-time event tracking and visual funnel analysis, allowing businesses to identify drop-offs, optimize conversion paths, and personalize experiences at scale. The company also enables dynamic cohort building, personalized campaign delivery, and feedback-loop integration with external ad platforms (e.g., Meta, Google), helping brands make more informed, data-driven decisions to maximize ROI and user engagement.
