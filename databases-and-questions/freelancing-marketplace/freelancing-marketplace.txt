Freelancing platform
Example: Upwork
This type of business operates a freelancing platform that connects employers and freelancers globally. Users can be differentiated between employers and freelancers. Employers can post jobs, each categorized and requiring specific skills. Freelancers can maintain a detailed profile with portfolio, education, experience and certifications. They can apply for the jobs that employers posted, which may lead to contracts. The wage is handles by different payment methods. After the completion of contracts, employers and freelancers can give reviews to each other.
